What We Cover

Where Biology Meets Compute

The overlooked signal in the biology. The causal framework that shouldn't work but does. The training trick, the generative model, the insight that reshapes what the next architecture needs to learn. Both sides of the bench welcome.

🧬

Genomics & Transcriptomics

Foundation models for DNA/RNA, language models for sequences, single-cell expression modeling.

🔬

Proteomics & Structure

Protein language models, structure prediction, intermolecular interactions, and multi-modal bio representations.

⚙️

Model Design & Training

Tokenization strategies, pre-training/fine-tuning regimes, architecture choices that actually matter.

🔗

Multi-Omics Integration

Bridging single- to multi-omics. Cross-modal learning. Making different data types talk to each other.

📊

Evaluation & Benchmarks

How do we know any of this works? Perturbation prediction, benchmark design, and honest assessment.

📝

Position Papers & "Why"

The hard questions. Why build foundation models for biology? What should they even do? We read the arguments.
